Modern frontends like Next.js, Svelte, Remix, and Astro present significant deployment challenges, which is why services such as Netlify and Vercel built specialized infrastructure. The post examines why contemporary frontends are hard to deploy, why AWS, GCP, and Azure offer inadequate support, and the recent advances that let SST provide enhanced frontend support.
